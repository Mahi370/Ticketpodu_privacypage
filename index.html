Privacy policy

Ticket Podu collects your Google sign-in details and the trip reminders you save so alerts sync across devices. We use Firebase to sign you in and store reminders. We do not sell your data or use it for third-party ads. Pro subscription status is stored on your account; payments are handled by Google Play.

Introduction

Ticket Podu ("we", "our", or "us") helps travellers set reminders for IRCTC, SETC, and other ticket booking windows. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when you use the Ticket Podu mobile app.

Information we collect

Account information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your display name, email address, and profile photo from your Google account through Firebase Authentication.

Reminder data: Trip details you save in the app — such as route, travel date, booking type, and alert preferences — are stored so your reminders work across sessions and devices.

Subscription data: If you purchase Ticket Podu Pro, we store your subscription status, plan type, and expiry information linked to your account so the app can enable or disable Pro features. Payment card and billing details are handled by Google Play, not by Ticket Podu.

Device and usage data: We may collect basic technical information needed to deliver notifications, sync data, and keep the app running reliably (for example, app version and sync timestamps).

How we use your information

We use your information to authenticate you, store and sync your reminders, verify Pro subscriptions, schedule local notifications and alarms, and improve app reliability. We do not sell your personal information.

Third-party services

Ticket Podu uses Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and related infrastructure) to sign you in and sync app data. Google's handling of data is governed by Google's own policies.

Ticket Podu Pro purchases are processed through Google Play Billing. Google collects and processes payment information according to Google Play's privacy policy and terms.

When you open official IRCTC, SETC, or other booking websites from the app, those sites operate under their own terms and privacy practices.

Data retention and security

Your reminder data is kept while your account is active so the app can function. You may delete reminders in the app at any time.

If your Ticket Podu Pro subscription ends and you have more than 3 reminders saved, reminders beyond your 3 earliest entries (by the time each was added) are deleted automatically from our systems, along with their scheduled alerts.

We apply reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Your choices

You can sign out at any time from your profile. You can revoke notification permissions in your device settings. You can manage or cancel a Pro subscription through Google Play → Subscriptions.

You can delete individual reminders in the Mine tab at any time without deleting your account. To request access to, correction of, or deletion of account-related data, see Delete account & data below.

Children's privacy

Ticket Podu is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes are published means you accept the updated policy.

Delete account & data

You can request permanent deletion of your Ticket Podu account and all data linked to it. Individual reminders can be removed anytime in the app without deleting your account.

  1. Open the Ticket Podu app and sign out from your profile (optional).
  2. Email supportticketpodu@gmail.com with the subject line: Delete my Ticket Podu account.
  3. Send the email from the same Google account you use to sign in to the app.
  4. We will process your request and delete your account within 30 days.

Data deleted: your display name, email address, profile photo, saved reminders, subscription status, notification preferences, and other app data linked to your account.

Data kept: Google Play billing and payment records are managed by Google Play, not Ticket Podu. Cancel an active subscription in Google Play → Subscriptions before or after requesting account deletion.

What Ticket Podu does

Ticket Podu is a reminder and planning tool for IRCTC, SETC, KSRTC, and other travellers. It helps you track booking windows and receive alerts before tickets open.

The free plan includes up to 3 booking reminders in the Mine tab. Ticket Podu Pro is an optional paid subscription that adds unlimited Mine reminders, IRCTC Tatkal reminders, and custom ticket types.

Payment, billing, renewals, cancellations, and refunds are handled by Google Play under Google's terms. You can cancel anytime in Google Play → Subscriptions. Mine reminders when Pro ends

When your Pro subscription ends or is not active, Pro-only features stop immediately. Tatkal and Custom ticket reminders no longer send notifications or phone alarms until you resubscribe.

If you have more than 3 reminders saved when Pro ends, only your 3 earliest reminders (by the time each was added to Mine) are kept. Any additional reminders are removed automatically from your account, along with their scheduled notifications and alarms.
