About

What is Ticket Podu?

Ticket Podu is a reminder and planning tool — not a ticket booking app. It tells you exactly when IRCTC, SETC, and KSRTC booking windows open, so you can book on the official websites yourself.

Indian train and bus tickets open on fixed schedules. Miss the window by even a few minutes and seats fill up fast. Ticket Podu watches those schedules for you, sends notifications and alarms, and keeps your trips organised in one dashboard.

Privacy policy

Last updated 11 July 2026

What we collect

Account: Google display name, email, and profile photo via Firebase Authentication.

Reminders: Route, travel date, booking type, and alert preferences you save in the app.

Subscription: Pro status and expiry linked to your account. Payments handled by Google Play.

Device: Basic technical info (app version, sync timestamps) for notifications and reliability.

How we use it

We authenticate you, sync reminders, verify Pro subscriptions, schedule notifications and alarms, and improve reliability. We do not sell your data or use it for third-party advertising.

Third-party services

Firebase for sign-in and data sync. Google Play Billing for Pro purchases. Official IRCTC, SETC, and KSRTC sites have their own privacy practices when you open them from the app.

Your choices

Sign out anytime. Revoke notification permissions in device settings. Manage Pro in Google Play → Subscriptions. Delete individual reminders in Mine without deleting your account.

Account

Delete account & data

Permanent removal of your account and all linked data.

Option 1 — In the app (fastest)

  1. Open Ticket Podu and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile → Delete my account.
  3. Confirm. Your account and data are removed immediately.

Option 2 — By email

If you cannot open the app, email supportticketpodu@gmail.com from your sign-in Google account with subject Delete my Ticket Podu account. Processed within 30 days.

What gets deleted

Display name, email, profile photo, all reminders, subscription status, notification preferences, and other app data linked to your account.

What we keep

Google Play billing records are managed by Google. Cancel active subscriptions in Google Play → Subscriptions.
