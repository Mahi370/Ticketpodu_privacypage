Privacy Policy

Ticket Podu · Last updated 11 July 2026

Introduction

Ticket Podu ("we", "our", or "us") helps travellers set reminders for IRCTC and SETC ticket booking windows. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when you use the Ticket Podu mobile app.

Information we collect

Account information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your display name, email address, and profile photo from your Google account through Firebase Authentication.

Reminder data: Trip details you save in the app — such as route, travel date, booking type, and alert preferences — are stored so your reminders work across sessions and devices.

Device and usage data: We may collect basic technical information needed to deliver notifications, sync data, and keep the app running reliably (for example, app version and sync timestamps).

How we use your information

We use your information to authenticate you, store and sync your reminders, schedule local notifications, and improve app reliability.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for third-party advertising.

Third-party services

Ticket Podu uses Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and related infrastructure) to sign you in and sync app data. Google's handling of data is governed by Google's own policies.

When you open official IRCTC or SETC websites from the app, those sites operate under their own terms and privacy practices.

Data retention and security

Your reminder data is kept while your account is active so the app can function. You may delete reminders in the app at any time.

We apply reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Your choices

You can sign out at any time from your profile. You can revoke notification permissions in your device settings.

You can delete individual reminders in the Mine tab at any time without deleting your account.

To request access to, correction of, or deletion of account-related data, see Delete your account below or contact us using the details below.

Delete your account

You can request deletion of your Ticket Podu account and associated data by following these steps:

1. Open the Ticket Podu app and sign out from your profile (optional).
2. Email supportticketpodu@gmail.com with the subject line: Delete my Ticket Podu account.
3. Send the email from the same Google account you use to sign in to the app.
4. We will process your request and delete your account within 30 days.

Data deleted: your display name, email address, profile photo, saved reminders, subscription status, notification preferences, and other app data linked to your account.

Data kept: Google Play billing and payment records are managed by Google Play, not Ticket Podu. Cancel an active subscription in Google Play → Subscriptions before or after requesting account deletion.

Children's privacy

Ticket Podu is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes are published means you accept the updated policy.

Contact us

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Email us at supportticketpodu@gmail.com.
