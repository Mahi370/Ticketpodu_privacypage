Common topics

Reminders not firing? Check notification and alarm permissions in your phone settings. Battery saver modes can delay alerts.

Pro not showing? Use Restore purchases in the app while signed in with the Google account used to buy Pro.

Wrong booking window? Schedules can change — always confirm on the official IRCTC or SETC website before booking.
