What Ticket Podu does

Ticket Podu is a reminder and planning tool for IRCTC, SETC, KSRTC, and other travellers. It tracks booking windows and sends alerts before tickets open — it does not book tickets for you. Features may include daily booking alerts, trip reminders, full-screen phone alarms, a festival calendar, an in-app notification inbox, and a home-screen widget.

Ticket Podu is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by Indian Railways, IRCTC, SETC, KSRTC, or any government transport authority.

Free plan: up to 3 reminders in the Mine tab, daily alerts, trip alarms, festivals, widget, and the invite program.
Pro plan: unlimited reminders, IRCTC Tatkal alerts, custom ticket types, and related notifications and phone alarms.
