What Ticket Podu does

Ticket Podu is a reminder and planning tool for IRCTC, SETC, KSRTC, and other travellers. It tracks booking windows and sends alerts before tickets open — it does not book tickets for you.

Ticket Podu Pro

Pro is an auto-renewing subscription via Google Play (1-month, 3-month, or 12-month plans). Prices are shown before payment. Billing, renewals, cancellations, and refunds follow Google Play's terms.

Cancel anytime in Google Play → Subscriptions. Pro features stay active until the end of your current paid period. Use "Restore purchases" on a new device when signed in with the same Google account.

When Pro ends

Pro-only features stop immediately when your subscription ends. Tatkal and Custom reminders no longer alert until you resubscribe.

If you have more than 3 reminders when Pro ends, only your 3 earliest (by date added to Mine) are kept. Extra reminders and their scheduled notifications are removed automatically without further notice.
