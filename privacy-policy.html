Privacy Policy

What we collect when you use Ticket Podu to set IRCTC, SETC, and KSRTC booking reminders.

Last updated 11 July 2026

Introduction

Ticket Podu ("we", "our", or "us") helps travellers set reminders for IRCTC, SETC, KSRTC, and other ticket booking windows. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when you use the Ticket Podu mobile app.

Information we collect

Account information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your display name, email address, and profile photo through Firebase Authentication.

Reminder data: Trip details you save — route, travel date, booking type (General, Tatkal, Custom), and alert preferences — are stored so your reminders sync across sessions and devices.

Subscription data: If you purchase Ticket Podu Pro, we store subscription status, plan type, and expiry linked to your account. Payment and card details are handled by Google Play, not Ticket Podu.

Device data: Basic technical info (app version, sync timestamps) to deliver notifications and keep the app reliable.

How we use your information

We use your data to sign you in, store and sync reminders, verify Pro subscriptions, schedule notifications and phone alarms, and improve reliability. We do not sell your personal information or use it for third-party advertising.

Third-party services

We use Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore) for sign-in and data sync. Pro purchases go through Google Play Billing. When you open IRCTC, SETC, or other official booking sites from the app, those sites follow their own privacy practices.

Data retention

Reminder data is kept while your account is active. You can delete individual reminders in the Mine tab anytime.

When Pro ends and you have more than 3 reminders, only your 3 earliest (by date added) are kept; extras are removed automatically with their scheduled alerts.

Your choices

Sign out from your profile anytime. Revoke notification permissions in device settings. Manage or cancel Pro in Google Play → Subscriptions.

Delete individual reminders in the Mine tab anytime. To delete your entire account, use Profile → Delete my account in the app, or see our Delete account & data page.

Children's privacy

Ticket Podu is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact

Questions? Email supportticketpodu@gmail.com or visit Support.
