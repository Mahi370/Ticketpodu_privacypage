Delete account & data

Request permanent deletion of your Ticket Podu account and everything linked to it.

Last updated 11 July 2026

Before you delete

Deleting your account removes all saved reminders, Pro status, and profile data from Ticket Podu. This cannot be undone.

If you only want to remove specific trips, open the app → Mine tab → delete individual reminders. No account deletion needed.

If you have Ticket Podu Pro, cancel your subscription in Google Play → Subscriptions to avoid future charges. Google Play keeps its own billing records separately from Ticket Podu.

How to request deletion

  1. Open Ticket Podu and sign out from your profile (optional).
  2. Email supportticketpodu@gmail.com with subject: Delete my Ticket Podu account.
  3. Send the email from the same Google account you use to sign in.
  4. We process requests and delete your account within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Display name, email address, and profile photo
  • All saved reminders (IRCTC, SETC, KSRTC, Tatkal, Custom)
  • Subscription status and notification preferences
  • Any other app data linked to your account

What we do not delete

Payment and billing history on Google Play is managed by Google, not Ticket Podu. Cancel active subscriptions in Google Play before or after your deletion request.
